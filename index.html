ISSessions CTF 2024

About

Dive into the world of digital espionage with ISSessions 2024 CTF. Unravel mysteries, crack codes, and outsmart your opponents in this thrilling CTF challenge.

The CTF will be held on January 13th to 14th, 2024. The CTF will be held in person as well as hosted on the ISSessions Discord server. Test your problem-solving and teamwork skills with a variety of challenges including web, forensics, crypto, and more. Perfect for hackers, developers, and digital enthusiasts ready for an espionage-themed adventure. The CTF is designed to be beginner-friendly while still offering more difficult challenges for advanced players. We will be giving out prizes to the top 3 teams. The prizes will be announced closer to the CTF date. We will also be giving out prizes for the best writeups.

Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply a cybersecurity enthusiast, this event is the perfect arena to showcase your skills, learn new ones, and network with like-minded individuals.

Registration

Registration for Espionage 2024 will be opening shortly. Stay tuned for the link to join this thrilling expierence. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned expert, we have challenges for everyone. Don't miss your chance to be part of this unique experience!

FAQ

+What is a CTF?

A CTF is a competition where teams compete to solve challenges that test their skills in a variety of areas including web, forensics, crypto, OSINT, pwn, and more.

+ Do I need to be an expert in cybersecurity to participate?

No! Espionage 2024 welcomes participants of all skill levels.

+Are there any prerequisites for joining?

There are no specific prerequisites required, just bring your laptop, 3 of your most talented friends, and your enthusiasm and willingness to learn!

+ How do I register?

Registration links will be going out shortly. You will need to provide your name, email, and discord username. You will also need to provide your team name and the names of your teammates. You can register as a team of up to 4 people. If you do not have a team, you can register as an individual and we will assign you to a team.

+ What are the prizes?

We will be giving out amazing prizes to the top 3 teams. The prizes will be announced closer to the CTF date. We will also be giving out prizes for the best writeups.
